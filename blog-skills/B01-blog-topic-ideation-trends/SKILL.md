# B01: Blog Topic Ideation & Trend Spotting

## Objective
Generate a steady pipeline of fresh, engaging blog topics in the visa/travel domain that:
- Appeal to Indian travelers (aspirational, practical, seasonal)
- Have proven search demand or social traction
- Differ from structured visa guide content (more narrative/experiential)
- Cover destinations, travel tips, cultural insights, packing, food, and more
- Feed into the blog content calendar (B10)

## Sources for Topic Discovery

### 1. Social & Community Signals
| Source | What to Look For | Example Query |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Reddit** (r/travel, r/india, r/solotravel, r/backpacking) | Recurring questions, complaints, "unpopular opinions" | "site:reddit.com/r/travel best time to visit Bali Indians" |
| **Quora** | Detailed travel questions with many upvotes | "What are hidden gems in Thailand for Indian tourists?" |
| **Instagram / Pinterest** | Trending travel aesthetics, hashtags, destination reels | #TravelIndia, #SoloTravelIndia, #BudgetTravel |
| **YouTube** | Viral travel vlog topics, "things to do" videos | "Things to do in Vietnam for Indians" |
| **Twitter/X Trends** | Travel-related trending topics, hashtags | #VisaUpdate, #TravelGoals, #BudgetTravel |
| **Facebook Travel Groups** | Indian traveler community discussions | "Indians in Europe Travel Group" |

### 2. Search & SEO Signals
| Source | What to Look For |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Google Trends** | Rising queries, breakout topics, seasonal peaks |
| **Google "People Also Ask"** | Question-based content gaps |
| **AnswerThePublic** | Preposition-based keyword ideas |
| **Ahrefs/SEMrush Content Gap** | Topics competitors rank for but you don't |
| **Google Discover** | Trending travel cards shown to Indian users |
| **Pinterest Trends** | Seasonal travel inspiration keywords |

### 3. Seasonal & Calendar Signals
| Season | Blog Topic Angles |
|--------|------------------|
| **Summer (Apr-Jun)** | Summer Europe trips, monsoon destinations, student travel |
| **Monsoon (Jul-Sep)** | Off-season travel, budget getaways, Kerala/Goa in rain |
| **Festival (Oct-Dec)** | Christmas markets, Diwali abroad, New Year destinations |
| **Winter (Jan-Mar)** | Snow destinations, visa application peaks, study abroad prep |
| **Holiday Breaks** | Dussehra trips, Diwali getaways, Christmas holidays |
| **Visa Policy Changes** | New eVisa countries, rule changes for Indians |

### 4. Competitor & Industry Signals
- Follow 5-10 top Indian travel blogs (Travel + Leisure India, Outlook Traveller, etc.)
- Monitor visa/immigration news sites
- Track airline route announcements (new direct flights from India)
- Track new hotel/resort openings in popular destinations
- Monitor budget airline sales and new routes

## Topic Categorization Framework

Classify each topic idea into one of these blog content buckets:

| Bucket | Description | Examples |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Destination Guides** | Experiential guides to places | "Bali on a Budget: Complete Guide for Indians" |
| **Travel Tips & Hacks** | Practical advice | "10 Things I Wish I Knew Before Flying to Europe" |
| **Cultural Deep Dives** | Local customs, food, etiquette | "Japan Etiquette Guide for Indian Tourists" |
| **Personal Stories** | First-person narratives | "My First Solo Trip to Thailand: What Went Wrong" |
| **Comparisons** | Head-to-head destination/choice | "Vietnam vs Thailand: Which is Better for Indian Travelers?" |
| **Seasonal / Event** | Time-bound content | "Best Places to Celebrate Diwali Outside India" |
| **Packing / Logistics** | What to bring, how to plan | "Ultimate Packing List for Europe in Winter (from India)" |
| **Money / Budget** | Cost analysis | "How Much Does a 7-Day Dubai Trip Actually Cost?" |

## Topic Scoring Matrix

Score each topic idea (1-5) on:

| Criterion | Weight | Why It Matters |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Search Demand** | 25% | Exists on Google Trends, has volume |
| **Social Buzz** | 20% | Being talked about on Reddit/IG/YouTube |
| **Seasonal Relevance** | 15% | Right time to publish |
| **Competition Gap** | 15% | Low competition but high interest |
| **Indian Audience Fit** | 15% | Relevant to Indian travelers specifically |
| **Monetization Potential** | 10% | Affiliate / sponsored / lead gen potential |

### Priority Labels
| Score Range | Label |
|-------------|-------|
| 4.0 - 5.0 | 🔥 HOT — Create immediately |
| 3.0 - 3.9 | 📈 WARM — Schedule within 2 weeks |
| 2.0 - 2.9 | 💤 COLD — Backlog for slow periods |
| Below 2.0 | ❌ DISCARD |

## Output
- Topic brief document with:
  - Proposed blog title (working title)
  - Content bucket category
  - Primary angle / hook
  - Target audience persona (from B02)
  - Sourcing notes (why this topic now)
  - Priority score
  - Suggested publish window (seasonal tie-in)

## Quality Check
- [ ] Topic is NOT something already covered by your visa guides (00-19)
- [ ] Topic has proven demand (search, social, or seasonal)
- [ ] Topic has a clear Indian traveler angle
- [ ] Topic fits into one of the content buckets
- [ ] Topic scored and prioritized
- [ ] Seasonal timing is correct (not past the window)
- [ ] Could be turned into a listicle, story, or comparison format
