# B10: Blog Content Calendar & Batch Planning

## Objective
Create a structured editorial calendar that ensures consistent, diverse blog output. Balance content types, leverage seasonal trends, and plan batch production for efficiency.

## Calendar Structure

### Monthly Theme Framework
Assign each month a broad travel theme to guide topic selection:

| Month | Theme | Rationale |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| January | **New Year Planning** | Resolutions, travel goals, visa planning |
| February | **Valentine's / Budget** | Couple travel, budget-friendly tips |
| March | **Spring Escapes** | Spring destinations, Holi abroad |
| April | **Summer Preps** | Summer destination guides, student travel |
| May | **Summer Peak** | Family travel, monsoon planning |
| June | **Monsoon / Off-Season** | Budget monsoon travel, student trips |
| July | **Europe Peak** | Europe guides, Schengen tips |
| August | **Festival Travel** | Independence Day trips, Onam, festivals |
| September | **Autumn Planning** | Autumn destinations, Dussehra trips |
| October | **Diwali Travel** | Diwali abroad, festive season getaways |
| November | **Winter Preps** | Winter destinations, year-end planning |
| December | **Year-End Wrap** | Christmas, New Year, annual reflections |

### Content Mix Ratio (Per Month)

| Content Type | % of Monthly Output | Frequency (for 12 posts/month) |
|-------------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| Destination Guides | 25% | 3 posts |
| Travel Tips & Hacks | 25% | 3 posts |
| Listicles & Comparisons | 17% | 2 posts |
| Personal Stories | 17% | 2 posts |
| Budget / Cost Analysis | 8% | 1 post |
| Seasonal / Event | 8% | 1 post |

### Weekly Cadence Template

| Day | Content Type | Notes |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Monday | Destination Guide | Weekend trip planning research |
| Wednesday | Travel Tip / Listicle | Practical mid-week utility |
| Friday | Story / Comparison | Engaging weekend reading |
| (Optional) Saturday | Budget / Seasonal | Weekend project content |

## Batch Production Schedule

### Weekly Batch Workflow (4 hours)

| Block | Time | Task |
|-------|------|------|
| **Block 1** | 60 min | Topic ideation (B01) for next week + persona assignment (B02) |
| **Block 2** | 90 min | Write 2 blog drafts (B03/B04/B05/B06/B08) |
| **Block 3** | 30 min | Visual briefs (B07) for both posts |
| **Block 4** | 30 min | Social snippets (B09) for both posts |
| **Block 5** | 30 min | Review + schedule |

### Monthly Batch Workflow

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Research & plan entire month's topics |
| Week 2 | Write & review drafts (4 posts) |
| Week 3 | Visuals, social snippets, formatting |
| Week 4 | Schedule, crosslink, publish |

## Content Health & Audit

### Monthly Review Checklist
- [ ] All 4 weekly posts published on time
- [ ] Content mix ratio maintained (per above targets)
- [ ] Each post targets a different persona (from B02)
- [ ] At least 1 post linked to a visa guide
- [ ] Social snippets created for all posts
- [ ] Top 3 posts shared to Pinterest
- [ ] Monthly traffic analytics reviewed (top/bottom performers)
- [ ] Next month's seasonal events added to calendar

### Quarterly Deep Audit (Every 3 Months)
- [ ] Which blog types performed best? (traffic/engagement)
- [ ] Which personas engaged most?
- [ ] Content gaps identified for next quarter
- [ ] Competitor content moves reviewed
- [ ] Seasonal content calendar for next 3 months updated
- [ ] Old blogs checked for freshness (updates needed?)
- [ ] Blog → news → guide crosslink opportunities identified

## Output
A complete content plan containing:
- Monthly theme and rationale
- 4-5 blog topics with titles, types, and personas
- Weekly publishing schedule
- Batch production plan (what to write when)
- Seasonal tie-ins and event-based content
- Visa guide crosslink opportunities per blog
- Monthly review checklist

## Quality Check
- [ ] Content mix ratio is balanced (not all same type)
- [ ] Personas are rotated across posts
- [ ] Seasonal/event content is timed correctly
- [ ] At least 2 posts link to visa guides (cross-sell)
- [ ] Publishing schedule is realistic (buffer days included)
- [ ] Batch production time budgeted
- [ ] Monthly review and quarterly audit scheduled
- [ ] Month's theme reflected in topics chosen
- [ ] Social repurposing (B09) time allocated in workflow
