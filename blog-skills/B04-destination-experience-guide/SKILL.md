# B04: Destination & Experience Guide Format

## Objective
Produce comprehensive, visually rich destination guides that help Indian travelers plan their trip to a specific city, region, or country. These are experiential guides — not visa procedural guides.

**Distinction from visa guides (08-content-generation):**
- Visa guides = How to get permission to enter
- Destination guides = What to do, see, eat, and experience once you're there

## Destination Guide Template

```markdown
# [Destination Name] Travel Guide for Indians [Year]: Cost, Best Time, Places & Food

## Output
A complete destination guide in markdown with:
- All sections from the template
- Indian-specific context (INR, food, cultural notes)
- Visual-friendly section headers (optimized for image placement)
- Internal links to related visa guides
- Budget tables in INR
- Sample itineraries (brief)
- FAQ section (8-12 questions from Indian travelers)
