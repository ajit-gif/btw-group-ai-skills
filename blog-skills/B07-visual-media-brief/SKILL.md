# B07: Visual & Media Brief Creation

## Objective
Create detailed visual briefs for every blog post so designers, photographers, or AI image tools can produce consistent, on-brand visuals that enhance the reader experience and improve social sharing.

## Visual Hierarchy for a Blog Post

```
Hero Image (Top of post — sets the mood)
├── In-Content Images (every 300-400 words — breaks text)
│   ├── Location / Experience photos
│   ├── Food photos
│   ├── Map screenshots
│   └── Screenshots (booking apps, websites)
├── Infographics (data, comparisons, budgets)
├── Pull-out Quote Graphics (shareable for social)
└── Social Media Cards (Pinterest, Instagram, Twitter)
```

## Visual Brief Template for Each Image

```markdown
### Image X: [Descriptive Title]

**Type:** Hero / In-content / Infographic / Social Card
**Placement:** After paragraph [X] / Section [name]
**Purpose:** [What this image should convey — mood, info, proof]

**Description:**
[2-3 sentences describing exactly what should be in the image]

**Reference Style:**
- Color palette: [e.g., warm tones, blues and whites, tropical greens]
- Mood: [e.g., peaceful, adventurous, luxurious, authentic]
- Composition: [e.g., wide shot, close-up, overhead, portrait]
- Lighting: [e.g., golden hour, bright daylight, moody evening]

**Text Overlay (if applicable):**
[Exact text to appear on image]

**Dimensions:**
- Blog post: 1200×630px (OG standard)
- Pinterest: 1000×1500px (vertical pin)
- Instagram: 1080×1080px (square) or 1080×1350px (portrait)
- Twitter: 1200×675px

**Alt Text:**
[Descriptive alt text for SEO/accessibility]

**Source:**
[AI Generate / Stock Photo / Custom Photography / Screenshot]
```

## Image Types & Requirements

### 1. Hero Image
- **Purpose:** First visual impression. Sets the emotional tone.
- **Style:** High-quality, aspirational, well-lit
- **Content:** Destination landmark, iconic experience, or stunning landscape
- **Text overlay:** Blog title (optional)
- **Key rule:** Must look premium even for budget travel content

### 2. In-Content Location Photos
- **Purpose:** Show the reader what a place actually looks like
- **Style:** Authentic, not overly staged
- **Content:** People experiencing the place (not empty landscapes)
- **Key rule:** Include people for relatability (Indian travelers preferably)

### 3. Food Photography
- **Purpose:** Make readers hungry and curious
- **Style:** Close-up, well-lit, showing texture and color
- **Content:** Local dishes, street food, restaurant ambiance
- **Key rule:** Show the dish + hand holding it (for scale and warmth)

### 4. Infographics
| Type | Content | Style |
|------|---------|-------|
| Cost Breakdown | Budget figures per category | Clean bar charts or pie charts |
| Comparison | X vs Y features | Side-by-side columns |
| Timeline | Day-by-day schedule | Horizontal timeline |
| Checklist | Pre-trip items | Icons + text boxes |
| Map | Route / areas to stay | Minimal map with pins |

### 5. Pull-Out Quote Graphics
- **Purpose:** Social sharing (quote cards)
- **Style:** Large text, minimal design, brand colors
- **Content:** Most impactful 1-2 lines from the post
- **Size:** 1080×1080px (Instagram-friendly)

### 6. Map / Location Graphics
- **Purpose:** Show spatial relationship between places
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, not cluttered
- **Content:** Marked points of interest, routes, neighborhoods
- **Tool suggestions:** Google My Maps, Canva, SnazzyMaps

## Image Generation Prompts (For AI Tools)

Use this format for tools like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Adobe Firefly:

```
Template:
"Travel photograph of [subject/scene] in [destination], [time of day] lighting,
[composition style], [mood/atmosphere], [camera style], Indian travelers visible,
warm color palette, ultra realistic, 8K --ar [ratio]"

Example:
"Travel photograph of a young Indian woman eating street food at a night market in
Bangkok, golden hour lighting, candid shot from slightly above, warm and bustling
atmosphere, authentic Thai street food visible, shallow depth of field, ultra
realistic, 8K --ar 16:9"
```

## Output
A complete visual brief document containing:
- Hero image brief (with reference style, composition, lighting)
- 3-5 in-content image briefs (locations, food, experiences)
- 1-2 infographic briefs (budget, comparison, timeline)
- 1 pull-out quote graphic brief
- 2-3 social media card briefs (Pinterest, Instagram, Twitter)
- AI generation prompts for each image
- Alt text for every image (SEO-optimized)
- Dimension specifications per platform
