## How to Assign Persona to a Blog Topic

For each blog topic (from B01), determine:

1. **Primary Persona**: Who is this MOST useful for?
2. **Secondary Persona(s)**: Who else would read this?
3. **Tone Adjustment**: Language tweaks needed for primary persona
4. **CTA Alignment**: Match CTA to persona's motivation
5. **Format Choice**: Pick format that suits this persona best

## Output
For each blog post, a persona brief containing:
- Primary persona name + profile
- Tone and voice guidelines for this specific piece
- Content format recommendation
- CTA recommendation tailored to persona
- Any persona-specific DO/DON'T notes

## Quality Check
- [ ] Persona selected before content creation begins
- [ ] Tone, format, and CTA all aligned with chosen persona
- [ ] Content avoids mixing personas (keeps a consistent voice)
- [ ] Pain points of chosen persona addressed in content
- [ ] CTA speaks to persona's primary motivation
