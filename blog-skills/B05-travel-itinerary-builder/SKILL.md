# B05: Travel Itinerary Builder

## Objective
Create practical, day-by-day travel itineraries that Indian travelers can follow as-is or adapt. Each itinerary balances attractions, food, rest, and travel time with clear cost estimates and logistics.

## Itinerary Types

| Type | Duration | Best For | Output Style |
|------|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Quick Trip** | 2-3 days | Weekend getaways, stopovers | Tight schedule, minimal travel between spots |
| **Classic Trip** | 5-7 days | Standard vacation | Balanced pace, mix of popular + hidden gems |
| **Deep Dive** | 10-14 days | Full exploration | Slower pace, day trips, local experiences |
| **Themed Trip** | Varies | Food, adventure, culture, photography | Focused on specific interest |
| **Budget Blitz** | Varies | Backpackers | Lowest-cost options, hostels, street food |

## Output
